More is More

from the collection of "clutter is design"

more images = less taken for granted

Images accumulate to prevent abstraction, insisting that things be seen rather than assumed.

more embeds = fewer experiences flattened

Time-based and external media remain intact instead of being summarized or reduced.

more attachments = less left documented

Files persist as formal traces, acknowledging that some information demands record rather than display.

more texts = fewer assumptions made

Text extends explanation beyond what is immediately visible, preferring over-clarification to misreading.
